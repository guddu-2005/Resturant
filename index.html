The Ancestral Heirloom

Crafting Heritage Since 2008.

The Alchemy of Chhena

Born from the sacred kitchens of Odisha and refined through the regal courts of Bengal, Chena Jhilli represents more than just a sweet; it is a cultural pulse. Our journey began with a single copper vessel and a commitment to preserving the “Jhilli” technique—a delicate balance of frying and syrup immersion that requires decades to master.

We don’t just cook; we curate an experience that honors the ancient Rangoli of flavors. Every dish is a testament to the artisans who came before us, ensuring that the warmth of marigolds and the scent of saffron are present in every bite.
